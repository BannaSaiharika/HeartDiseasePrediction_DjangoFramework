Symptoms

Coronary artery disease

Symptoms are Chest pain or discomfort(angina), Shortness of breathe or trouble breathing, Feeling dizzy or lightheaded, Heart palpitations, Feeling tired, Nausea, Stomach discomfort or vomting, Weakness

High Blood pressure

Symptoms are Severe headaches, Chest pain, Dizziness, Difficulty breathing, Nausea, Vomting, Blurred vision, Anxiety, Nosebleed, Fatigue or confusion, Irregular heartbeat, Blood in the urine.

Cardiac arrest

Symptoms are Sudden loss os consciousness, Lack of pulse, No breathing, Dizziness or chest pain it causes by an electrical malfunction in the heart that caused an irregular heartbeat(arrhythmia).

Stroke

Symptoms are Numbness or weakness in the face, arm, or leg.Confusion om trouble speaking or understanding speech, Trouble seeing in one or both eyes, Trouble walking, dizziness, or problems with balance.

Causes

Cornary artery disease

A buildup of fatty plaques in the arteries(atherosclerosis) is the most common cause of coronary artery disease. Risk factors include a poor diet, lack of exercise, obesity and smoking. Healthy lifestyle choices can help lower the risk of atherosclerosis.

High Blood pressure

Some factors that can increase the risk of high bp are Being overweight, Eating too much salt, Not getting enough exercise, Smoking, Drinking too much alcohol or coffee, Having lot of stress, Being over 65.

Cardiac arrest

The main cause of cardiac arrest are Hypoglycemia, Anaphylaxis, Hyperthermia, Hypothermia, Tension pneumothrax, Drug abuse, Smoking, Stress, Sedentary lifestyle.

Stroke

The risk factors are Being overweight or obese, Physical inactivity, Heavy or binge drinking, Use of illegal drugs such as cocaine and methamphetamine, Diabetes, High cholesterol.
